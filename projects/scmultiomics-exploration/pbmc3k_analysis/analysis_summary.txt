BIOLOGICAL INTERPRETATION:
-------------------------
The PBMC 3k dataset shows a typical peripheral blood mononuclear cell composition:
- Lymphoid cells (T cells, NK cells, B cells): ~82.5%
- Myeloid cells (Monocytes): ~16.7%

The high proportion of CD4+ T cells is consistent with healthy PBMC composition.
NK cells show strong cytotoxic markers (NKG7, GZMA, GNLY).
Monocytes express typical inflammatory markers (LYZ, S100A9).
B cells show clear immunoglobulin and HLA class II expression.
